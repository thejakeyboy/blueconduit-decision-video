Two questions place any utility — unknowns? lead/GRR? — and galvanized splits the "no known lead yet" case. Everyone gets Mains — by the deadline and beyond.

| What's underground | Before Nov 2027 | Beyond |

## Who we're for (and who we're not)

We want customers with ongoing needs — and we use price to find them.

* Track 1 — just the 2027 deadline: capture it, price it right, nudge them to commit. Price bundles that yield discounts if committing now.  
* Track 2 — lead \+ beyond and Track 3 — water mains: convert and keep. This is the business.  
* The lever: commit through 2028 → a lower annual rate. The buyer who won't commit shows they're Track 1 — and pays the premium.

For No Lead, price may be high; but discounted in bundle with Mains today.  
For regular SL inventory, price includes full platform by default since optimizing replacements comes included, but discount if commit through Nov 2028 for 2-Year contract. Otherwise, higher price.
